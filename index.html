    Premier Higher Education Institution

    The University of the Cordilleras is a premier Higher Education Institution in the Philippines. UC is awarded by CHED with Autonomous Status (since 2010), University Status (since 2003), and Centers of Excellence (since 2006). It is an identified Delivering HEI for Faculty Development, S&T and SIKAP Scholarships, and recipient of Research and Creative Project Grants.
